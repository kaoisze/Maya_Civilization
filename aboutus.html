Background Information

Reasoning For Creating Site

This site was created because we wanted to give useful information regarding Maya civilization before major European contact. The reason why this is important is because the Maya were an ancient civilization and knowing the past of indigenous people can greatly benefit us to being open-minded and a different point of view than our own. We have shown this through Maya geography, religion, technology, art, and more.
