Technology

The Mayas were innovators and were one of the most advanced ancient civilizations during their time.


Notable Accomplishments

The Mayans were well-versed in astronomical sciences, and used it to created a calendar that consisted of three parallel cycles: the sacred system, the secular system and the Long Count.

In addition, their efforts in astrology & mathematics guided them immensley to predict upcoming events to better crop harvest and influenced many other notable things in religion.

Lastly, their efforts with architecture is something monumental that still stands reminiscent of the civilzation that lived in Mesoamerica.



Language

Before 2000 BC, the Maya spoke Proto-Mayan which is the ancestor of the other 30 Mayan languages. Many theorize that the original Proto-Mayan homeland originated in the Guatemalan Highlands.


Medical Knowledge

Writing & Numerical System

The Maya writing system, which looks like hieroglyphics is one of the most monumental achievements of those in Mesoamerica. Furthermore, it was the most advanced writing system among more than a dozen that evolved in Mesoamerica.

The Maya used a base 20 (vigesimal) system. The Maya adopted it by the Late Preclassic and added the symbol for zero, which is thought to be one of the earliest examples.
