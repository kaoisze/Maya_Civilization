Geography


Location

The Mayans civilization was located in Central America.

The location of their civilization located in present-day Guatemala, southern parts of Mexico, Belize and in western Honduras and El Salvador. Their descedents currently live in area where their ancestors were centuries ago.

Climate

The Maya civilization was divided into two areas, the highlands and lowlands. The northern portion of the lowlands had high temperatures and there was a lack of water sources which led to many droughts. The only sources of water were sinkholes called cenotes.

The southern Maya highlands is a mountainous reigon that is located in present day Guatemala, and most prominent is that the location has a volcano which is the highest point in Central America.
